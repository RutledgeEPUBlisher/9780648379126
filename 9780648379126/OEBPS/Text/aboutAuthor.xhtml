About the Author

Troy Anthony Woolls Rutledge

Troy Anthony Woolls Rutledge, author of this publication, is the great-great-grandson of the James Rutledge whom departed Ireland for Australia in 1839. I lived in Belfast, Northern Ireland, for 1 year to live and work, and to conduct research. I preserve the life of my fore-bearers for the benefit of the my son, Edwin, heir to the name Rutledge.
